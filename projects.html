University projects

Would it be a university degree if there wasn't a whole lot of group projects? I don't know. In any case, I have gotten the great opportunity to work on some really cool projects throughout my studies. While my bachelor's degree in media included lots of stuff regarding communication, my master's degree studies have more so been about UI/UX testing and prototyping.

The most recent finished project I worked on was a study pal -finding mobile app prototype that worked like a popular dating app (I'm not sure if I'm allowed to say the name, but it rhymes with cinder). Like most of the projects I've worked on, it was created from scratch on Figma, with one other student working with me. At the same time I was a part of another project, where we created a study location finder mobile app, once again on Figma.

I think it's safe to say that I have gained a great deal of experience with working in diverse groups as well as with using Figma. As I mentioned briefly, my background in media allowed me to work on my communication and teamwork skills, and I believe those work beautifully in the UI/UX developmenting field. No one really works alone, and it's good to know how to talk to people. You knew that already though.
